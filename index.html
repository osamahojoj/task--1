Pizza

This homemade pizza is crispy, cheesy, and full of flavor.
Perfect for family dinner or a weekend treat!

Ingreients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into dough and let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out the dough and place on a baking tray.
  4. Spread tomato sauce and add cheese + toppings.
  5. Bake in preheated oven at 220°C for 15–20 minutes.
